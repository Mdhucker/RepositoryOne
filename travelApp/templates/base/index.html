About Us

Welcome to Mishan Tours & Travel Company

Here at Zanzibar's premier Tours Travel company we specialize in Day and excursions daily!
These include Jozani Forest, MI Island, Prison Island and so much more!.

In addition to our tours, we also provide reliable and friendly transfer services 24 hours a day across the island.

At Mishan Tours, we strive for excellence and try to do things in a way that has never been done before.

That's why we showcase our little Tropical Island in the Indian Ocean in a sustainable way while exploring Nature, Culture, and Local Communities.
